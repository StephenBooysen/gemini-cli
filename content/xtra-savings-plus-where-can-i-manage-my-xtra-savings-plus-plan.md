# Where can I manage my Xtra Savings Plus Plan? 

- Navigate to your profile on Sixty60 by tapping on the profile icon on the top right of the home screen.  

- Tap ‘My Xtra Savings’ 

- Go to the Xtra Savings Plus tab to manage your plan.
